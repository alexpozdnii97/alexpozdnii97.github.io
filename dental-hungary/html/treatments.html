Dental Implants

Single Dental Implant

Single Dental Implants can be used to replace a missing tooth permanently. First, your dentist will surgically place the implant in your jawbone. Once it fuses to the bone, the implant serves as the new “root.

Multiple Dental Implant

The power of multiple dental implants is that they give you realistic teeth on the minimal number of dental implants. This means that multiple dental implants are a very cost-effective option.

All - On - 4

The All-On-4 dental implants is a procedure where the surgeon places four implants into the maxilar bone providing a film foundation.

All - On - 6

The All - On - 6 dental implants technique is based on the procedure of placing 6 implants in the upper jaw and 6 implants in the lowe jaw.

Crowns

When a tooth is cracked, decayed or damaged, a crown may need to be filted onto the teeth. A crown is a permanent covering that fits over the original tooth.

Dental Bridges

Replace missing tooth or damaged teeth with dental bridges.

Zirconium Crown and Bridge

Replace missing, decayed or ugly teeth with beautiful zirconium crowns. These crowns won’t decay or break, they have a natural look and metal free for a smile makeover.
