About Tose Zafirov

Bodybuilder, Mr. Olympia Representative, Businessman, Husband, Father

The Role of Fitness On My Journey

I was born in Negotino, Macedonia, on the 22nd of January, 1978, and started my bodybuilding journey at 17. Fitness has helped me improve my body function and productivity, pursue my everyday tasks more effectively, and keep a positive mindset.

I have worked with several fitness trainers, and I owe each of them a lot, especially Mauro Sassi, a hardworking individual, friend, and the person responsible for my biggest achievement, earning a medal on the Mr. Olympia stage.

My vision is to motivate others to follow my steps and achieve greatness because fitness isn’t something limited to professionals – it’s for everyone on this planet!
