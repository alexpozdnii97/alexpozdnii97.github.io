STEP 1

Initial Consultation

Your 1-on-1 fitness journey will always start with a consultation with your personal trainer. You can schedule it via text, phone, email, or video. Each coach handles this stage differently. You’ll be asked to share your medical history, fitness background and experience, desired program length, workout preferences, and health and fitness goals.

STEP 2

Developing Training Program

Your online trainer will create a personalized diet and workout plan following the personal information you have shared. As you progress through your transformation, your trainer will adjust your program to better address your progress and push you towards the end goal more efficiently. You’ll be informed and consulted before any changes occur.

STEP 3

Put Your Personalized Plan Into Action

Once everything is prepared, it’s time to get to work. You’ll be in constant communication with your trainer, who will keep track of your progress. You’ll have information and guidance readily available at your fingertips to help you improve your daily performance.
