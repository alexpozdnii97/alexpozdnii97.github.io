About Gladiator Gym

Gladiator Gym is one of Macedonia’s fastest-growing, result-oriented fitness centers that helps people achieve their fitness goals. It is located in Kisela Voda Municipality and has been operational since 2007. We have cardio, weight loss, and muscle gain equipment, which is well suited for individuals with different fitness goals.

Yet, our biggest asset is our team of experts, all of which have significant accomplishments in the fitness world. You’ll always have someone to help you, demonstrate how to use a machine, or advice you further regarding your fitness journey. Gladiator Gym is the place of winners, the perfect spot to find the needed motivation to change your life for the better!
